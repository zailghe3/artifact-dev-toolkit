Summarize the meeting transcript for a busy operator.

Inputs:
- Transcript or notes:
- Meeting purpose:
- Attendees:

Output:
1. Three-line summary.
2. Decisions made.
3. Action items with owner and due date.
4. Open questions.
5. Risks or blockers.

Use clear bullets. If owner or date is missing, mark it as unassigned or TBD.
